Dog - Golden Retriever

The Golden Retriever is one of the top ten most popular dogs in the U.S. It's all good with the Golden: he's highly intelligent, sociable, beautiful, and loyal.

  • Dog Breed Group: Sporting Dogs
  • Height: 1 foot, 9 inches to 2 feet tall at the shoulder
  • Weight: 55 to 75 pounds
  • Life Span: 10 to 12 years

The breed’s friendly, tolerant attitude makes him a fabulous family pet, and his intelligence makes him a highly capable working dog. Golden Retrievers excel at retrieving game for hunters, tracking, sniffing out drugs, and as therapy and assistance dogs. They’re also natural athletes, and do well in dog sports such as agility and competitive obedience. Read more at this reference.
